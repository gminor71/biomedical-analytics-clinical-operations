# Decision Log Template

This template supports documentation of key project decisions in regulated,
cross-functional, and delivery-oriented environments.

## Purpose

Use this log to:
- document important project decisions
- capture rationale and approval context
- support traceability for scope, timeline, and operational changes
- maintain a record for governance and audit readiness

## Instructions

- Use one row per decision
- Record the decision only after it has been confirmed or approved
- Capture the rationale clearly and briefly
- Link the decision to its source where possible

## Field Definitions

- **decision_id**: unique identifier
- **decision_title**: short name for the decision
- **decision_date**: date approved or finalized
- **category**: Scope, Timeline, Resource, Operational, Vendor, Regulatory, Statistical, Other
- **description**: summary of the decision
- **rationale**: reason supporting the decision
- **approved_by**: approver or governance body
- **impacted_area**: workstream or function affected
- **implementation_owner**: person or function responsible for execution
- **status**: Approved, Pending Implementation, Implemented, Superseded
- **source**: meeting, review, governance forum, or related document
- **notes**: follow-up details or dependencies

## Recommended Usage Notes

- Use this log for decisions with operational, timeline, compliance, or delivery impact
- Avoid mixing proposed options with confirmed decisions
- Link major decisions to related RAID items when relevant
