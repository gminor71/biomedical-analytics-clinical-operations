# Site Overview

Site 003 is the highest enrolling site in the trial with 14 subjects enrolled at
the time of this assessment. The site has demonstrated strong recruitment 
capability and cooperative engagement with the study team.

Higher enrollment volume increases operational workload and monitoring attention.

confirmed operational deficiencies.
