# Risk Assessment

Site: Site 003
Visit: Monitoring Visit 2 (MV2)
Timeline Phase: t2 – Active Monitoring

---

## Overall Risk Level

**Medium — volume-driven with emerging operational trends**

---

## Risk Summary

Site 003 remains a high-enrolling and operationally engaged site. 
Source documentation is generally complete, and no critical compliance 
issues have been identified.

However, compared with the previous monitoring visit, there is evidence o
f recurring operational patterns that warrant continued attention.

---

## Key Risk Drivers

### 1. Data Entry Timeliness

Delays in EDC data entry (approximately 4–6 days post-visit) persist and are consistent with findings from the previous monitoring visit.

**Risk Interpretation:**
At present, delays are moderate and appear related to workload rather than data availability. Continued persistence may impact data review timelines if not addressed.

---

### 2. Query Management

A subset of queries originating from the previous monitoring visit remains open beyond expected resolution timelines.

**Risk Interpretation:**
Emerging pattern of delayed query resolution suggests potential strain on site resources.

---

### 3. Adverse Event Documentation

Adverse event narratives show improvement but continue to require clarification in some cases.

**Risk Interpretation:**
While not critical, inconsistent documentation may affect data quality and requires continued monitoring.

---

### 4. Visit Window Compliance

A second minor visit window deviation was identified.

**Risk Interpretation:**
Currently low impact; however, recurrence indicates a potential developing trend.

---

### 5. Treatment Administration Documentation

A treatment delay was identified with incomplete rationale documented in the EDC.

**Risk Interpretation:**
Isolated at this stage, but requires clarification to ensure alignment with protocol and source documentation.

---

## Comparison to Baseline and Previous Visit

* **Baseline (t1):** Risk driven primarily by high enrollment volume
* **MV1:** Initial operational impacts observed (data entry delays, documentation gaps)
* **MV2:** Recurring patterns now evident across multiple domains

These observations indicate a transition from isolated findings to emerging operational trends.

---

## Comparison to Other Sites

Relative to other sites in the study:

* Site 003 demonstrates higher enrollment performance
* Query volume is slightly elevated but remains manageable
* Data entry timeliness is slower than lower-enrolling sites

Overall performance remains acceptable but reflects workload-driven variability.

---

## Risk Trend

**Stable with early upward pressure**

Risk remains at a medium level; however, persistence of current trends may lead to increased risk if not addressed.

---

## Recommended Monitoring Approach

* Continue routine monitoring frequency
* Maintain focus on data entry timeliness and query resolution
* Monitor for recurrence or escalation of current patterns
* Reassess risk level at next monitoring visit

No escalation is required at this time.
