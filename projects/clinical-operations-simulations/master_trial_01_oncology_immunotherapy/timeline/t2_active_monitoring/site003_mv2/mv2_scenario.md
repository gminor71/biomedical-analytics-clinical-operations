active monitoring.

- review follow-up from MV1

---

## Data Review Observations

### Query Environment

Site 003 now has approximately **9 open queries**.

Most remain minor and relate to:

- AE clarification
- visit date confirmation
- incomplete documentation fields
- treatment administration rationale

Two queries from the previous monitoring cycle remain open longer than expected.

---

### Data Entry Timing

Source documentation remains generally complete and organized.

However, several recent visits were entered into EDC **4–6 days after visit completion**. This represents a continued delay compared with expected data entry timeliness.

The pattern appears related to workload rather than absence of source documentation.

---

### Visit Timing

Most visits remain within protocol-defined windows.

One additional visit occurred **3 days outside the target window**, again attributed to scheduling constraints. At this stage, this remains a minor issue but will require continued review if repeated.

---

## Safety Review

A small number of additional treatment-emergent adverse events have been reported since MV1.

Reported events continue to include common low-grade oncology treatment symptoms such as:

- fatigue
- nausea
- mild infusion-related symptoms

No serious adverse events have been reported at this site.

One subject had a documented treatment delay associated with laboratory review, but the rationale in the EDC requires clarification to ensure consistency with source documentation.

---

## Operational Observations

Positive indicators:
- strong enrollment continues
- source documentation remains mostly organized
- site staff are cooperative and responsive

Emerging concern:
- increased enrollment volume is beginning to affect timeliness of data entry and query resolution

This does not yet represent a major compliance issue, but it suggests rising operational pressure.

---

## Initial Monitoring Impression

Site 003 remains a productive and operationally engaged site. Current findings are still manageable and largely consistent with high subject volume.

Compared with MV1, the site shows modest improvement in some documentation areas but persistent pressure in:

- data entry timeliness
- query turnaround
- minor scheduling variation

The monitoring focus is shifting from isolated findings to recurring operational patterns.
