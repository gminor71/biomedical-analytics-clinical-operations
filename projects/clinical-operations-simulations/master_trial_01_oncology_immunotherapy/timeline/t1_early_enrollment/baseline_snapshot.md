# Early Enrollment Baseline Snapshot

**Timeline Phase:** t1_early_enrollment

---

## Purpose

This document defines the operational starting state of Master Trial 01 at the beginning of active CRA monitoring.

It represents the baseline environment from which future scenarios and operational developments will evolve.

This is not a scenario and does not represent a list of findings.

---

## Trial Timing Context

* Approximate study age: **8–10 weeks after First Patient In (FPI)**
* Sites activated and enrolling
* Early treatment cycles underway
* Initial monitoring activities beginning

## General Operational Health (Trial-Level)

Overall trial status is considered:

**Low to Medium operational complexity (expected for early enrollment).**

Observed patterns include:

* Routine query generation beginning
* Minor visit scheduling variability
* Early safety event reporting appearing
* Normal differences between sites

No major systemic concerns identified at baseline.

---

## Data Entry & Query Environment

Typical expected conditions:

* Small number of open queries per site
* Most queries related to clarifications or missing data fields
* Query aging generally within acceptable range
* Site 003 showing slightly higher query volume due to enrollment pace

This is considered operationally normal.

---

## Safety Environment

* Early treatment-emergent adverse events (TEAEs) reported.
* No confirmed trial-wide safety signal at this stage.
* Sites demonstrating variable experience with immunotherapy documentation.

Minor inconsistencies in narrative detail may exist but are expected early.

---

## Monitoring Expectations at Baseline

The CRA is expected to:

* Establish normal performance benchmarks.
* Observe early site behaviors and patterns.
* Provide coaching where appropriate.
* Differentiate normal variability from emerging risk trends.

Focus is on understanding baseline conditions rather than escalation.
