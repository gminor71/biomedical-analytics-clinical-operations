# Data Review Observations

Initial monitoring review identifies mostly routine findings.

### Query Environment

Approximately **6 open queries** associated with Site 003.

Typical issues include:

* missing lab units
* incomplete AE narrative text
* visit date clarification

These are consistent with early study activity.

---

### Visit Timing

Most visits fall within protocol windows.

One Cycle 2 visit occurred **2 days outside the visit window**, but documentation indicates scheduling constraints.

This is not considered a major deviation at this stage.

---

### Data Entry Timing

Source documentation appears complete.

However:

* some data entered into EDC **3–5 days after visit completion**

This is slightly slower than ideal but common early in study execution.

---

### Safety Review

A small number of treatment-emergent adverse events have been reported.

Examples include:

* fatigue
* mild infusion reactions
* nausea

All events currently classified as **Grade 1–2**.

No serious adverse events reported at this site.

# Operational Observations

Site staff appear engaged and responsive.

Positive indicators:

* faster data entry following visits.

# Initial Monitoring Impression

Continued monitoring will focus on:

* adherence to visit windows as enrollment increases.
