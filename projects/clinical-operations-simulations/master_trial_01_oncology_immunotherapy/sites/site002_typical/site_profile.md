# Site002 – Typical Site

Site002 represents a typical oncology research site with moderate enrollment capacity and standard operational variability.

Characteristics:

* steady but slower enrollment compared with high-performing sites
* small study team with shared responsibilities
* occasional delays in data entry due to competing site workload

Operational Environment:
Site002 is expected to perform adequately but may occasionally require clarification queries or minor follow-up during monitoring visits.

Monitoring Focus:

* verify timely data entry following visits
* review completeness of adverse event documentation
* monitor adherence to protocol visit windows

Risk Profile:
Low to moderate operational risk. Issues identified at this site are expected to be routine and manageable through standard monitoring activities.
