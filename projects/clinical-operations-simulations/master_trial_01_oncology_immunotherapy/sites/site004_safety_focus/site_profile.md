# Site004 – Safety-Focused Site

Site004 represents a site where safety monitoring requires increased attention due to patient population complexity and early treatment-emergent adverse events.

Characteristics:

* moderate enrollment pace
* experienced investigator with strong clinical oversight
* patient population with higher baseline comorbidities

Operational Environment:
Due to the clinical characteristics of enrolled subjects, Site004 may report a higher number of adverse events compared with other sites in the trial.

Monitoring Focus:

* confirm accurate grading of adverse events
* verify timely safety reporting
* review documentation of treatment modifications or dose holds

Risk Profile:
Moderate safety-related oversight requirements. No compliance concerns are expected, but the site may generate more safety documentation requiring monitoring review.
