# Endpoint Summary — Master Trial 01

## Primary Endpoint

### Objective Response Rate (ORR)

Definition:

Percentage of subjects achieving:

* Complete Response (CR), or
* Partial Response (PR)

based on investigator tumor assessments.

Operational considerations:

* Imaging timing must follow protocol schedule.
* Missing or delayed scans may impact endpoint assessment.
* Consistent documentation of response evaluations is required.

---

## Secondary Endpoints

### Safety and Tolerability

Assessment of treatment-emergent adverse events (TEAEs), including:

* Severity grade
* Seriousness
* Relationship to treatment
* Actions taken (dose hold, reduction, discontinuation)

Operational focus:

* Timely AE/SAE documentation
* Accurate linkage between safety events and dosing decisions

---

### Duration of Response (DoR)

Time from first documented response until progression.

Requires:

* Accurate imaging dates
* Consistent response classification

---

### Progression-Free Survival (PFS) — Simplified

Time from enrollment to disease progression or death.

Simulation emphasis:

* Imaging schedule adherence
* Progression documentation consistency

---

## Operational Relevance

Endpoints in this simulation are included primarily to:

* Connect clinical operations decisions to downstream analysis.
* Demonstrate how operational quality influences data integrity.
* Support later statistical analysis exercises within CT projects.
