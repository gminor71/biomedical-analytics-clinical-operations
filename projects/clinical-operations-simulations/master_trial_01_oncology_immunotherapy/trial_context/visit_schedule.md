# Visit Schedule — Master Trial 01

## Overview

This document provides a simplified operational visit schedule for the oncology 
immunotherapy simulation.

The schedule is intentionally streamlined for training purposes while reflecting 
common oncology Phase II workflows.

---

## Study Periods

### 1. Screening Phase

* Informed consent
* Eligibility confirmation
* Baseline labs
* Baseline imaging

**Window:** Up to 28 days before first dose.

---

### 2. Treatment Phase (Q3W Cycles)

Treatment cycles occur every **3 weeks (21 days)**.

Typical assessments per cycle:

* Physical exam
* Performance status assessment
* Safety laboratory testing
* Adverse event review
* Concomitant medication review
* Dose administration

---

### 3. Imaging Schedule

Tumor assessments:

* Baseline imaging
* Every 6 weeks during early treatment
* Every 9–12 weeks thereafter (simulation simplified as every 2 cycles)

Imaging timing is operationally important because delays may affect endpoint 
evaluation.

---

### 4. End of Treatment

Occurs when:

* Disease progression
* Unacceptable toxicity
* Withdrawal of consent
* Investigator decision

Assessments include:

* Final safety review
* Treatment discontinuation documentation

---

### 5. Follow-Up (Simplified)

* Safety follow-up after treatment discontinuation
* Survival status collection (not fully simulated)

---

## Operational Notes

* Visit window adherence is monitored.
* Late visits may represent protocol deviations depending on severity.
* Lab timing and AE assessments are critical for dosing decisions.
* Imaging timing impacts efficacy evaluation.

---

## Simulation Guidance

During scenarios:

* Visit timing may drift slightly (normal operational variation).
* Not all visit window variations represent major findings.
* Recurrent timing issues may contribute to increased site risk.
