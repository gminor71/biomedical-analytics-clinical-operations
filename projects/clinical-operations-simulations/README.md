# Clinical Operations Simulations

Scenario-based simulations designed to practice Clinical Research Associate
monitoring workflows and clinical trial operational decision-making.

Simulations model realistic trial environments including:

- site monitoring
- query management
- risk assessment
- monitoring documentation

Current Simulation
- Master Trial 01 – Oncology Immunotherapy

Simulations evolve through timeline phases representing real study progress.

Status: Early development – MV1 monitoring scenario completed.
